# Commercial licensing for Home-Automation (HV3.0) firmware

This repository is licensed under CC BY-NC 4.0 (see `LICENSE`) and is available for personal, non-commercial use only. If you plan to use this firmware in a product intended for sale or any other commercial activity, you must obtain a commercial license from the author before distribution.

How to request a commercial license
1. Preferred method (recommended):
   - Open a new issue using the `Commercial license request` template at `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/commercial-license-request.md`.
   - Use the issue title: `Home-Automation (HV3.0) Commercial License Request`.
2. Please provide the following information in your request:
   - Business / organization name
   - Contact person and contact email
   - Intended product and usage details
   - Whether you will modify the firmware (and a brief description of changes)
   - Estimated distribution volume (per month / year)
   - Target geographic regions
   - Any desired support, warranty, or contractual terms
3. The maintainer will review the request and reply with licensing options, negotiation, and (if applicable) a written contract and pricing.

Response time & privacy
- Please allow up to 7 business days for an initial response; enterprise requests may take longer.
- Do not include private or sensitive data in the public issue (e.g., passwords, private keys). The maintainer may follow up using the contact email provided in your issue for private discussions and contracting; contact details in an issue may be used to communicate privately.

Important notes
- The CC BY-NC 4.0 license does not prevent you from requesting a commercial license; it only requires permission from the author for commercial use.
- Do not distribute this software commercially until you have a signed commercial license from the author.
- This file is informational only. Final license terms, pricing, and contracts will be handled separately (offline).
